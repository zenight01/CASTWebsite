About Us

Ang layunin ng CAST ay makagawa ng makataong lipunan sa pamamagitan ng gawaing panteatro na makapagpamulat sa kabataang Pilipino upang masubukang lutasin ang suliraning kinahaahrap ng ating lipunan.

The Collective Arts of Students and Thespians (CAST) is a theatre organization that seeks to develop humane society by means of arts and culture as a tool for empowerment and contribution in community building.

The Collective Arts of Students and Thespians (CAST) is a theatre organization that seeks to develop humane society by means of arts and culture as a tool for empowerment and contribution in community building.

Vision

Makalikha ng kabataang S.A.L.T. o Student Artiat Leader Trainer.
To develop Student-Artist-Leader-Trainer or S.A.L.T..

Objectives

The objectives of this organization are as follows:
A. Train and expose the youth in all aspects of Philippine theater work so that they can perform relevant plays that can enlighten them and make them catalysts of change in the University of Makati and nearby communities;
B. Create a family of cultural workers aiming to popularize and give importance to culture and arts as a venue in attaining their dreams for themselves, their community and their country;
C. Mold the youth as leaders and role models with community organizing capabilities;
D. to help students develop proficiency in theater and cultural work.
E. to encourage students to enhance themselves through artistic and academic activities;
F. to evolve and create a form of expression uniquely Makate√±o which is deeply rooted from the experiences of the community;
G. Establish a network of artists from different institutions and organizations outside the university in the attainment of its Vision and Mission (NCCA, CCP, PETA, ALYANSA INC., NYC, Y4R, ATD 4th world, Tanghalang Bagong Sibol, TFDP, etc.); and
H. Raise funds that will support the projects and activities of the organization.
